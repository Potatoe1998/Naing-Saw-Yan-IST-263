Solar power

Solar power is energy harvested from the sun. Solar panels are usually mounted at a slanted angle to allow for maximum contact with sunlight for a maximum duration of time. Solar power is cheaper to install and maintain as compared to regular power lines.

Wind power

Wind power is energy harvested from the wind. Motors are usually mounted at a optimum angle to allow for maximum interaction with air currents for a maximum duration of time. Wind power is cheaper to install and maintain as compared to regular power lines.

Hydro power

Hydro Power is harvested through moving water It is a relatively inexpensive technology for homeowners to invest in
